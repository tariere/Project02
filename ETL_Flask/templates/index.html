NYC Airbnb

We took an AirBnB dataset from Kaggle and we have provided some additional ways to visualize the data to assist a user who is considering visiting NYC and wants to use AirBnB.

Where in NY am I? This visualization provides information about the listings and places it on a map so that user can get a better understanding of where they are located in the city. Once users narrow their selection, they can click on a link to get taken to the listing.
This visualization allows an exploration into the types of listings that are available in different boroughs and neighborhoods in NYC. If you hover on them, you can see the details of that category. Additionally, if the mood strikes, you can export the graph as a PNG or other file types!
An innovative visualization that allows users to perform a deep-dive into various facets of the data to explore and compare the listings.
